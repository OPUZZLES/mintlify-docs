AI-powered tutoring, also known as intelligent tutoring systems (ITS), refers to educational software that uses artificial intelligence to provide personalized instruction and feedback to students. These systems aim to mimic the benefits of one-on-one human tutoring while offering scalability and accessibility that traditional methods cannot match.

---

1. Ignition, allowing AI tutors to better understand and respond to students' emotional states.
2. Integration with virtual and augmented reality for immersive learning experiences.
3. Collaboration between AI tutors and human teachers to create blended learning environments.

---

### Conclusion

AI-powered tutoring represents a significant step forward in educational technology. While it may not entirely replace human teachers, it has the potential to supplement traditional education methods and make quality instruction more accessible to learners worldwide. As the technology evolves, we can look forward to increasingly effective and engaging AI tutors that help students reach their full potential.
