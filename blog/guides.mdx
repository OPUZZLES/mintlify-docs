## Getting Started

1. **Assess your current study habits** - Identify what's working and what needs improvement
2. **Choose appropriate techniques** - Select methods that align with your learning goals
3. **Create a study schedule** - Implement consistent practice with spaced intervals
4. **Track your progress** - Monitor improvement and adjust techniques as needed
